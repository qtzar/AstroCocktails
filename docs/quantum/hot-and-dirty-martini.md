---
title: Hot n Dirty Martini
description: ''
spirits:
  - Gin
liqueurs:
  - Dry Vermouth
ingredients:
  - 2 oz Gin
  - 1/4 oz Olive Juice
  - 2 Dashes Hot Pepper Bitters
  - Dry Vermouth
styles:
  - Style 1
glassware: Martini
method: Stirred
rating: 2.5
source: ''
---

- Mist glass with Dry Vermouth
- Stir with ice
- find strain to martini glass
- Garnish with 3 jalapeno stuffed olives
