---
title: Espresso Martini
spirits:
  - Vodka
description: Bitter, bold, and the ultimate pick-me-up cocktail.
ingredients:
  - 2 oz Vodka
  - 1 oz Fresh Espresso
  - 0.75 oz Coffee Liqueur
  - 0.25 oz Simple Syrup
glassware: martini
rating: 4.5
method: Shaken
---

Shake hard with ice.  
Strain into a chilled martini glass.  
Garnish with three coffee beans.
