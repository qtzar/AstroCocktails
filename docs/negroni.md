---
title: Negroni
spirits:
  - Gin
  - Vermouth
description: A bitter-sweet Italian aperitif classic.
ingredients:
  - 1 oz Gin
  - 1 oz Campari
  - 1 oz Sweet Vermouth
---

Stir all ingredients with ice until well chilled.  
Strain into a rocks glass over a large cube.  
Garnish with an orange twist.
