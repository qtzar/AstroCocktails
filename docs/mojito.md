---
title: Mojito
spirits:
  - Rum
description: Minty, fizzy and endlessly refreshing.
ingredients:
  - 2 oz White Rum
  - 1 oz Lime Juice
  - 0.75 oz Simple Syrup
  - 6-8 Mint Leaves
  - Soda Water
---

Muddle mint with syrup and lime in a glass.  
Add rum and ice, then top with soda.  
Garnish with a mint sprig.
