---
title: Lavender Lemon Drop
description: ''
spirits:
  - Vodka
ingredients:
  - 1.5 oz Tito's Handmade Vodka
  - 3/4 oz Lavender Syrup
  - 3/4 oz Lemon Juice
  - 1/4 oz Simple Syrup
styles:
    - Refreshing
glassware: Coupe
method: Shaken
rating: 2.5
source: Tito's
---

- Add all ingredients to shaker with ice.
- Shake and fine strain to a chilled coupe.
- Garnish with a lemon peel or lavender sprig.
