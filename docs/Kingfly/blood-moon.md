  - Gin

  - Orange Liqueur

  - 1.5 oz Kingfly Gin
  - 1/2 oz Kingfly Orange Liqueur
  - 1 oz Apple Cider

  - Sweet

- Dirty Dump in to a rock glass
- Garnish with orange peel.
