---
title: Classic Chocolate Martini
description: ''
spirits:
  - Vodka
  - Kahlua
ingredients:
  - 2 oz Vodka
  - 1 oz Kahlua
  - 1 oz Hershey's Chocolate Syrup
glassware: Martini
rating: 4
styles:
  - Chocolaty
method: Shake
---

- Decorate the inside of a martini glass with some chocolate syrup.
- Shake with ice.
- Fine strain in to the martini glass
