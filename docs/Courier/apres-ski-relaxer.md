---
title: Apres Ski Relaxer
description: ''
spirits:
  - Rum
ingredients:
  - 2 oz Spiced Rum
  - 1 oz Pineapple Juice
  - 1 oz Lime Juice
  - 1/5 oz Spiced Pecan Syrup
  - Grated Nutmeg
glassware: Rocks
rating: 2.5
styles:
  - Fruity
  - Warm
  - Winter
method: Shaken
source: Cocktail Courier
---

- add all ingredients to shaker with ice
- Strain to rocks glass with fresh ice
- Garnish with grated nutmeg
