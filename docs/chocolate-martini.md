  - 2oz vodka
  - 2oz Baileys Irish cream
  - 2oz Godiva chocolate liqueur

glassware: martini
styles:
  - Creamy
method: Shaken
---

Rim glass edge in chocolate syrup and decorate inside of glass.

Shake over ice and double strain in to glass. Garnish with shaved chocolate.
