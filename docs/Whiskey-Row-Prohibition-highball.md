---
title: Prohibition Highball
description: ''
spirits:
  - Bourbon
ingredients:
  - 1.5 oz Old Forester 1920 Bourbon
  - 1/2 oz Oleo Saccharum Syrup
  - Soda Water
styles:
    - Warm
    - Woodsy
glassware: Highball
method: Stirred
rating: 2.5

- Combine all ingredients in highball glass and stir.
- Garnish with a fresh lemon peal.
