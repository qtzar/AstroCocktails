---
title: Long Island Iced Tea
spirits:
  - Vodka
  - Gin
  - Rum
  - Tequila
  - Orange Liqueur
description: Boozy, bold and surprisingly balanced.
ingredients:
  - 0.5 oz Vodka
  - 0.5 oz Gin
  - 0.5 oz White Rum
  - 0.5 oz Tequila
  - 0.5 oz Triple Sec
  - 0.75 oz Lemon Juice
  - 0.5 oz Simple Syrup
  - Splash of Cola
method: Shaken
---

Shake everything except cola with ice.  
Strain into a highball glass over fresh ice.  
Top with cola and garnish with lemon.
