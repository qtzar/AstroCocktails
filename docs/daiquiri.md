---
title: Daiquiri
spirits:
  - Rum
description: Clean, crisp and perfectly balanced.
ingredients:
  - 2 oz White Rum
  - 1 oz Lime Juice
  - 0.75 oz Simple Syrup
glassware: hurricane
rating: 3.5
method: Shaken
---

Shake all ingredients with ice.  
Strain into a chilled coupe.  
Garnish with a lime wheel if you like.
