---
title: Gold Rush
description: ''
spirits:
  - Bourbon
ingredients:
  - 1.5 oz Old Forester 1870 Bourbon
  - 1/2 oz Honey Simple Syrup
  - 1/2 oz Lemon Juice
  - 2 Dashes Smoken Cinnamon Bitters
styles:
  - Light
  - Soft
glassware: Rocks
method: Shaken
rating: 2.5
source: Whiskey Row
---

- Combine all ingredients in shaker with ice
- Strain over fresh ice
- Garnish with lemon peel
